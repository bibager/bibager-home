About This Service

Bibager uses SMS to deliver automated notifications from its projects, including trade alerts from RZE, contact reminders from Personal CRM, and workflow notifications from N8N.

Messages are sent only to the account owner and are strictly one-way automated alerts. No marketing messages are sent, and your phone number is never shared with third parties.

Messages are delivered via Twilio. Message and data rates may apply.
